Thoughts on college

College is a hub of smart and ambitious people.

I arrived to Columbia with my ego up the roof. I was the best in my small 200 people high school in Beirut. In my first CS class, I barely knew how to code while others already knew how to set up servers in C. This really put me in my place and made me challenge myself throughout these 4 years.
